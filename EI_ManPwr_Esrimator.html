Parametric Model Documentation

Purpose: This parametric model adjusts base estimates using industry-specific factors to provide more accurate manpower and cost estimates.

  • Productivity Factor: Adjusts estimates based on team experience and efficiency (0.5-1.5)
  • Onboarding/Offboarding: Accounts for time needed to integrate new team members
  • Leave Rate: Compensates for vacation, sick leave, and other absences
  • Documentation Effort: Additional time for as-built drawings and redlines
  • Client Interaction: Time allocated for meetings, reviews, and approvals
  • Contingency: Buffer for unforeseen project risks and changes
